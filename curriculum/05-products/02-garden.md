# Garden

The human-agent workspace. Garden is where people and agents collaborate.

## What it does

Garden gives users a persistent personal agent with connected tools, shared skills, workflows, approvals, and a unified workspace. It is designed to raise the floor without lowering the ceiling — non-technical users get an easy product, power users keep deeper capabilities.

## How it fits

Garden gives the agent a workspace. It is the most user-facing product and the primary place where collaboration happens.
