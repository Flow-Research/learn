# Demo Week

At the end of each fellowship cycle, contributors present what they built during Demo Week.

## What to present

- A product contribution you made — code, docs, tests, or research.
- What problem it solves and how it fits into the value engine.
- What you learned and what you would do next.

## Format

Presentations are live and open to the whole community. They can be technical deep-dives, live demos, or walkthroughs of submitted work. Recordings are published so the wider community can watch.

## Why it matters

Demo Week is the capstone of each cycle. It turns individual contributions into shared knowledge, lets contributors get feedback from the full team, and builds the habit of presenting your work in public — a core skill for anyone building in open source.
