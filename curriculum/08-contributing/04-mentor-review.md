# Mentor Review

All contributions go through a mentor review process before points are awarded.

## How review works

1. A mentor is assigned to review your submission.
2. They verify the work is complete, original, and meets the contribution guidelines.
3. Feedback is provided — revisions may be requested.
4. Once accepted, the contribution is entered into the public ledger.

## What mentors look for

- Clarity and completeness of the submission.
- Whether the work meets the stated requirements.
- Evidence of understanding — not just output, but reasoning.
- Reusability — can others build on this?

## After approval

Approved contributions are recorded in FlowLedger. Points are credited, and the entry becomes part of your public contribution history.
