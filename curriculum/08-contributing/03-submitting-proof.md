# Submitting Proof of Work

Every contribution must be submitted with proof for mentor review and point allocation.

## What counts as proof

- Pull requests merged to a Flow Research repository.
- Documentation or lessons written and reviewed.
- Research notes, diagrams, or analysis published in the knowledge base.
- Test results, benchmark data, or audit reports.
- A working prototype or demo recording.

## How to submit

1. Complete your contribution.
2. Post a summary and link to the proof in the designated Discord channel.
3. A mentor will review and either approve or request changes.
4. Once approved, the contribution is logged in FlowLedger and points are awarded.

## Quality guidelines

- Proof must be verifiable — link to a PR, a published document, or a recording.
- Contributions should be complete enough for someone else to understand and build on.
- Duplicate or low-effort submissions will be returned for refinement.
