# Points, Badges, and Reputation

FlowLedger tracks contributor reputation through points, badges, and a public leaderboard.

## Points

Every approved contribution earns points. The point value depends on the scope and impact of the work:

- Small fixes or improvements — 1–5 points
- Feature work or new content — 5–20 points
- Major contributions or research — 20–50 points

## Badges

Badges mark milestones and special achievements:

- **First Contribution** — your first approved submission.
- **Consistent Contributor** — contributions across multiple weeks.
- **Product Specialist** — deep work in a specific product area.
- **Mentor** — reviewed and guided other contributors.

## Leaderboard

A public leaderboard shows top contributors by points, badges, and recent activity. It resets or rolls by cycle — details are published in the fellowship channel.

## Reputation

Your contribution history on FlowLedger serves as a verifiable record of work. It can be shared with projects, teams, or employers as proof of real open-source contribution.
